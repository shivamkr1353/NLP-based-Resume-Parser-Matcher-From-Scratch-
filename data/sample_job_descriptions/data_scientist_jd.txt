Senior Data Scientist

Company: TechVision Analytics
Location: Bangalore, India (Hybrid)
Experience: 3-5 years

About the Role:
We are looking for a Senior Data Scientist to join our AI team. You will lead the development of machine learning models that drive business decisions across our product suite.

Responsibilities:
- Design, build, and deploy machine learning models for production use
- Perform exploratory data analysis on large-scale datasets
- Develop NLP pipelines for text classification and sentiment analysis
- Build recommendation systems using collaborative filtering
- Create data visualizations and dashboards for stakeholders
- Mentor junior data scientists and conduct code reviews
- Write technical documentation and present findings to leadership

Requirements:
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Computer Science, Statistics, or related field
- 3-5 years of experience in data science or machine learning
- Strong proficiency in Python and SQL
- Hands-on experience with TensorFlow or PyTorch
- Experience with scikit-learn, pandas, numpy, matplotlib
- Knowledge of NLP techniques (tokenization, TF-IDF, word embeddings)
- Experience with cloud platforms (AWS or GCP)
- Familiarity with Docker and CI/CD pipelines
- Strong problem-solving and communication skills

Preferred Qualifications:
- Published research in ML/AI conferences
- Experience with deep learning (CNN, RNN, Transformer models)
- Knowledge of MLOps and model monitoring
- Experience with Spark or distributed computing
